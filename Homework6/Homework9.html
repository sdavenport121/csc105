Introduction to Pusheen

Pusheen is a happy, lazy, friendly kitty. He's got it all: friends, snacks, and naps!

Pusheen's Friends

Pip: Pusheen's littermate. Pip has long, fluffy dark hair.

Best quality: a dry sense of humor

Sloth: Pusheen's college roommate. Sloth is tall and enjoys margaritas.

Best quality: a ridiculously good memory

Stormy: Pusheen's other littermate. Stormy has curly hair that is gray like Pusheen's.

Best quality: never leaves kitty litter in the bed

Cheek: Pusheen's neighbor and business partner. Cheek is tiny but mighty!

Best quality: remembers to put the lid back on the Nutella so it won't dry out

Bo: Pusheen's mail-bird. Bo delivers all of Pusheen's mail!

Best quality: hides surprise snacks in the mail each morning

Pusheen's Favorite Snacks

  • Chips: crunchy, crispy, salty
  • Nutella and Pretzels: Sweet with a little bit of salty crunch
  • Cheez Curls: Cheesy, curly, and orange!
  • Pizza: Pepperoni Pizza is the one and only pizza for Pusheen!

Pusheen's Passion for Naps

Napping is an integral part of Pusheen's life. Pusheen likes taking naps with his brothers, Pip and Stormy. Pusheen also enjoys naps alone in a variety of places. Whether it's under an umbrella on the beach, flat on his back in the middle of the floor, or out in a sunny field, Pusheen can always find a way to get comfy and avoid the stress of being a modern-day kitty.
