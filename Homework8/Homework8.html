My To-Do List

Welcome to your personalized To-Do List webpage! To add a task to your to-do list, enter a new task and hit enter or click the Add Task button. Once you've added two or more tasks, you can search for tasks in your list using keywords. To show the entire to-do list again, click the Show List button. To Complete a task, press the Complete Button, and to Remove a task from the list completely, press the Remove Button. Enjoy!
